\subsection*{1. Milne's Method}

Use the quadratic interpolant to $y'(t) = f(t,y(t))$ at $t_n,t_{n-1}, t_{n-2}$ to obtain the formula (for $n\geq 3$)

$$ y_{n+1} = y_{n-3} + \frac{4h}{3}\left(2y_n' - y_{n-1}' + 2y_{n-2}' \right) + \mathcal{O}(h^5) \;. $$
